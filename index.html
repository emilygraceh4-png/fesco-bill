About FESCO:

Faisalabad Electric Supply Company (FESCO) is responsible for distributing electricity to several districts in Punjab, Pakistan. Known for efficient services, FESCO covers both urban and rural regions.

Coverage Areas

FESCO provides electricity in areas including Faisalabad, Jhang, Toba Tek Singh, Chiniot, and Sargodha.

Bill Information

Fesco Peak Hours

To reduce energy costs, it's recommended to avoid electricity usage during FESCO peak hours, typically from 6:30 PM to 10:30 PM.

How to check FESCO Bill?

You can easily check your FESCO duplicate bill using your 14-digit reference number. The process is straightforward and accessible via the official FESCO website or bill checking portals.

New Connection or Transfer Procedure:

Applications for new connections or ownership transfers can be submitted online or through FESCO customer service centers. Necessary documents include a copy of CNIC, property papers, and an undertaking form.

Taxes in Fesco Bill:

FPA:

Fuel Price Adjustment (FPA) is a charge based on fluctuations in fuel prices used in electricity generation.

TR Surcharge:

Tariff Rationalization surcharge aims to balance the tariff rates across various DISCOs in the country.

FC Surcharge:

Financing Cost (FC) surcharge helps cover the debt servicing costs of the power sector.

Deferred Amount:

This amount, if shown, can be paid later and does not carry a late payment surcharge if unpaid.

QTR Tariff Adj / DMC:

This quarterly adjustment ensures accurate recovery based on power purchase prices and distribution margins.

FAQs

Q: Can I download a FESCO duplicate bill?
A: Yes, you can download or print a FESCO duplicate bill by entering your reference number on official portals.

Q: Is the online bill valid for payment?
A: Absolutely. A printed version of the duplicate bill is valid for payment at banks and mobile apps.
